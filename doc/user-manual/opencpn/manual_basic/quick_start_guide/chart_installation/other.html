Chart Alternatives

This topic is out of scope for the Basic User Manual. See Advanced User Manual > Advanced Charts.
OpenCPN has the capacity to read other file types such as:

MBtiles

Refer to Advanced User Manual > Advanced Charts > MBTiles & other formats.

These are great MBTile Resources and we encourage others to add their MBTile downloads.

  • sv Ocelot’s MBtile Downloads

    * Charts and website by Jon Hacking
    * Coverage of the entire coastlines of most countries (with the exceptions of mainland Papua New Guinea, eastern Thailand, eastern Sumatra, and eastern Madagascar).
    * Over 30 countries, including all of the Eastern Caribbean, Galapagos, Micronesia, Solomon Islands, Papua New Guinea, most of tropical SE Asia, and much of the tropical Indian Ocean.
    * Satellite imagery from ArcGIS, Bing (MSFT), & Google Earth, as well as **nautical charts**  from C-Map (new, not CM93) & Navionics.
    * Satellite imagery has max positional errors on the order of 3m (10’) and most of them can see underwater reefs to about 10m depth (33’).
    * Resolution (Z18) is high enough to see individual coral heads, and they’re exactly where plotted.
    * Nautical charts aren’t as accurate, but they include depths, which are certainly useful.
    * How to use “Chart Groups” to switch between charts with only a single keystroke in OpenCPN. by Jon Hacking.

  • sv Migration’s The Chartlocker for MBTiles

    * Charts and website created by Bruce Balan & Alene D. Rice.
    * Pacific: Pacific Mexico, French Poly, Pitcairn Island, Easter Island, Central S Pacific, Western South Pacific, New Zealand, Japan, Taiwan, Western North Pacific,
    * Pacific (new): Cook Islands, Rapa Nui & Pitcairn Islands, Samoa & American Samoa, Tonga, Niue, Minerva Reefs & Beveridge Reef, Tokelau, Tuvalu, Wallis & Futuna
    * Atlantic: Bermuda, Bahamas, Turks & Caicos, parts of East Caribbean, etc.

  • sv Amazing Grace’s Charts

    * West Coast USA and Canada, California to Alaska. New in 2023.

  • sv Soggy Paws' Satellite MBTiles

    * Red Sea, Papua New Guinea, Solomon Islands, Philippines and Eastern Indonesia, Micronesia (west of Marshalls, including Palau), Marshall Islands, between Fiji and the Marshall Islands, Fiji, Tonga, Cook Islands, Society, San Blas

* sv Zen Again’s KAP Charts

    * Pacific Ocean, SE Asia, Crossing to S Africa, S Atlantic, Brazil (older)

  • sv Valhalla’s Charts-Terry’s Topics

    * Original site was discontinued, but Soggy Paws is maintaining links.
    * Read the included PDF file for the links to downloads.
    * SE Asia, Indian Ocean, and Western Pacific.
    * CM93 v2 (2007, 2015) Download for OpenCPN.
    * Anchorage waypoints in .gpx format
    * Many additional cruising topics

  • sv Jacaranda’s Charts-Houlihans

    * French Polynesia

CM93

Ask on Cruiser’s Forum or refer to Advanced User Manual > Advanced Charts > Chart Formats.

CM93 charts by C-Map were first produced in 1993 for professional mariners, e.g. cargo ships. As such, they do not address very well many of the places cruisers venture, and the resolution and details may be lacking. Some are very old versions, e.g. 2005, and C-Map switched from V2 to V3 charts in 2011, adding encryption.
